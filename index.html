    A little bit about myself...

    My name is Lleyton Seymour, an Australian-born American-based designer passionate about front-end development and interactive design! I recently graduated from High Point University with a dual degree in Computer Science and Electronic Media Production and am now attending NC State for a master's in Art + Design.

    When I'm not coding, you can find me taking photos or working on one of my art projects. For inquiries, commissions, and other questions, feel free to email me directly: contact@lleytonseymour.com
